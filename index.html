Our Story

Nature-First
Philosophy

Founded in 2006, Verdant was built on a simple belief: great landscapes start with deep respect for the land itself. We study each property's soil, light, water, and microclimate before a single plant goes in the ground.

Our team of certified horticulturists and landscape architects bring artistry and ecological thinking together — creating spaces that are beautiful today and sustainable for decades.

🌍
Eco-Conscious

Native plants, organic inputs, and water-wise design at the core of every project.

🏅
Certified Experts

Licensed landscape architects and ISA-certified arborists on every team.
